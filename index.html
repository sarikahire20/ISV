Smart Village

The 3 Pillar

To provide electricity, solar powered street lights, information about some key applications, knowledge about new digital trends through STEM lectures to make villagers self-reliant ,a step towards making 'ATMANIRBHAR BHARAT'.

Focus

To provide sustainability and networking to the target site/selected site along with clean energy solution for power outage issue and to fetch GSM Network.

Proposed Technologies

From Solar Panels for renewable energy to vermicomposting based on IOT to smart farming kits.. We are determined to implement knowledge for the society.

Ongoing Phase

Consists of an agreement with villagers and government authorities for their approval.
